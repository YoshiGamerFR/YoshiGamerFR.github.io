What is Waluigi's Fried Chicken? (WFC)





WFC is a joke game I made because joke games are funny. WFC is a really goofy game, if you want to play my games, I recommend starting of with this one for sure! WFC is filled with memes and other things, without it being too much. WFC was also a kind of tribute to one of my project I had to abandon, because at the time, it was too difficult for me to make, I still don't have the ressources to make it. I originally wanted to make a game called Five Nights at Waluigi's, because I didn't know about Five Nights at Wario's, it would have been going to be a series of games called FNaW, yeah... Just like Five Nights at Wario's...
